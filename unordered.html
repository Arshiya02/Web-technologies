Features of Popular Social Media Applications

Facebook

  • News Feed with personalized content
  • Friend requests and connections
  • Photo and video sharing
  • Groups and Pages
  • Messenger for instant messaging
  • Events creation and management

Instagram

  • Photo and video sharing
  • Stories feature for ephemeral content
  • Direct messaging
  • Explore page for discovering new content
  • IGTV for longer-form videos
  • Filters and editing tools

Twitter

  • Short-form text posts (tweets)
  • Retweets and quote tweets
  • Hashtags for trending topics
  • Lists for organizing follows
  • Direct messaging
  • Moments for curated content

LinkedIn

  • Professional networking
  • Job postings and applications
  • Skill endorsements
  • Article publishing
  • Business pages
  • Learning platform

TikTok

  • Short-form video creation and sharing
  • Duets and Reactions
  • Trending sounds and challenges
  • Live streaming
  • For You Page (FYP) for personalized content
  • Video editing tools and filters
