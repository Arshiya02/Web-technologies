Overview

Welcome to Mohan Babu University, where academic excellence meets real-world relevance. Our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that nurtures intellectual growth, fosters innovation, and promotes global excellence.

Programs We Offer

  • Undergraduate Programs: Start your academic journey with a strong foundation.
  • Postgraduate Programs: Advance your knowledge and skills in your chosen field.
  • Doctoral Programs: Engage in pioneering research and contribute to global knowledge.

Our Achievements

  • NIRF Rankings: Ranked in the 150-200 band.
  • QS-IGauge: Secured the coveted Diamond band.
  • Accreditations: NAAC A+ and NBA accreditations, along with a platinum category distinction in the AICTE-CII survey.

A Legacy of Excellence

From a young village boy to a teacher, actor, producer, writer, Member of Parliament, philanthropist, and educationist, our founder's journey is a testament to the power of dreams and dedication. Since its inception in 1992, the Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Trust (SVET) has helped thousands of young learners fulfill their aspirations.
